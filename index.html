The Vita Programme

Strategic Relocation to Italy

  • €300K flat tax coordination — A fixed annual payment covering all worldwide income for up to 15 years. Extended to family members at €25,000 each. Updated from €100K under the 2024 Budget Law reform.

    Art. 24-bis TUIR — The regime applies to individuals who have not been Italian tax residents for at least 9 of the prior 10 years. Italian-source income remains subject to standard rates.

  • Zero inheritance tax on foreign assets during the 15-year regime
  • IVIE & IVAFE exemptions — No wealth tax on foreign real estate or financial assets
  • Golden Visa structuring — Investor visa from €250K with full Schengen access
  • Property & renovation oversight — Trusted construction partners across all regions
  • Municipal navigation — Direct relationships with local authorities and former officials
  • Impatriati regime — 50% income exemption for skilled professionals relocating

The Helvetia Programme

Structured Residency in Switzerland

  • Pauschalbesteuerung — Lump-sum taxation calculated on annual living expenses (typically 7x rental value), not worldwide income. Available to non-Swiss nationals who do not engage in gainful employment in Switzerland.
  • No federal inheritance or gift tax — Most cantons exempt close relatives entirely
  • 26 cantons of fiscal sovereignty — Each with distinct, optimisable tax structures
  • CHF stability — Sound currency, institutional discretion, and banking excellence
  • Treaty optimisation — Leveraging Switzerland's extensive double-tax treaty network
  • Private banking introductions — Pre-screened relationships with Pictet, Lombard Odier, Julius Baer, and EFG International
  • Cross-border telework — Structured remote-work arrangements from Italy under the bilateral CH-IT telework framework, avoiding dual-taxation triggers

Key cantons Ticino · Zug · Schwyz · Zurich · Geneva · Vaud · St. Moritz
